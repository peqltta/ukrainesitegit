ABOUT TEAM BLACK CAT

TEAM BLACK CAT is a international creative collective with 30+ artists all over the globe. ‍ It was founded May 2020 in Zurich with the soul purpose of spreading love, support / inspire other creatives and form a openminded family that can elevate any scale of project to the next level.  We specialize in 3D & 2D multimedia creation with a wide variety in styles.

THERE ARE STORYS TO BE TOLD

Hello dear artists and NFT enthusiasts, you are most likely aware of the current situation in Ukraine or Russia, and perhaps you are unsure about what information is right or wrong these days. Many of us are devastated, mostly because it seems so difficult to find an answer to the following question: How can I really help?

The reason we wanted to curate this project is because the current situation impacts all of us. There are members in our collective with families in the Ukraine as well as in Russia. Our clients, partners, and other artists we know are affected by this war – whether it’s the displacement and destruction wrought on their families and friends, or the devastating impact to their financial and mental well-being. To be in a different country during these circumstances causes us to feel powerless and we believe many people are suffering in the same way.

We hope to encourage artists everywhere to take advantage of our freedom of speech, because our art can make a difference. Our art and creativity matters. It is powerful, it brings us together, and it can certainly save lives, especially now.

HOW WE WILL HELP

With this collection, we’re giving artists a chance to express their feelings about the war in Ukraine through their art. The art will then be made available for purchase as NFTs and the earnings will be donated directly to a number of European and Ukrainian aid organizations. These organizations provide medical healthcare, shelter, and food to the people most affected by the war. We also want to make sure to donate a portion of the proceeds to civilians who are risking their lives to transport medicine, food, hygiene products, and clothes to the Ukrainian borders.Of course our project won’t stop this war but every cent counts for these people. For the artists participating, the most important aspect to us is the message we communicate. This collection will amplify those messages so our voices can have an impact and our art can make a difference. Make art not war.
